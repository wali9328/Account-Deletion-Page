OctoShare – Account & Data Deletion Request

AG Software House respects user privacy and data protection rights. This page explains how users can request deletion of their OctoShare account and associated personal data.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your OctoShare account and personal data, please send an email from your registered email address to:

Email: support@octoshare.app

Please include the following details:

  • Your registered email address
  • App name: OctoShare
  • Subject: Account & Data Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • User account identifiers
  • Earnings and reward records
  • Bandwidth usage records associated with the account

Retention Period

Deletion requests are processed within 7–30 days after identity verification. Some data may be retained temporarily if required by law or fraud prevention obligations.
